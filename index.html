History of URS
Pililla Campus

The University of Rizal System Pililla is one of the leading external campuses that provides quality education and training to the youth of Pililla and its neighboring towns. Its existence was conceived because of the growing request from the residents of the Municipality to have their own college institution that could offer affordable and quality education and would primarily cater to the needs of high school graduates of the town. This was realized when Rizal College of Agriculture and Technology (RCAT) was formally opened on May 22, 1991 through the efforts of Former Mayor Nicomedes F. Patenia, Dr. Heracleo D. Lagrada, The President of RCAT and Prof Apolinario F. Masinsin, the first designated Campus Director with the approval of the Mayor, the old municipal building was used as the first school site for almost four years, until, this campus was moved in Sitio Dampol, Brgy. Bagumbayan, Pililla, Rizal.
